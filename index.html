    What is a Pomodoro Timer?

    A Pomodoro Timer is a productivity technique where you break your work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks. This method is designed to improve focus and productivity by encouraging frequent short breaks.

    Typically, you work for one Pomodoro (25 minutes) and then take a short break (5 minutes). After completing four Pomodoros, you take a longer break (15-30 minutes).
